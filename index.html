Anastasias' favorite Video Games

I like many different types of video games, but my most favorite are horror/alt story telling games!

  • The Walking Dead
    • is my favorite game that is a mix of both horror and story telling! Its a dramatic and heartfelt game based on a zombie apocalypse where your decisions can either kill or save a life

  • The Last Of Us
    • is another zombie apocalypse but a bit different. This game is also a dramatic and heartfelt story, with more violence and horror. This game is a Third person shooting game where you can see your character and have the free will of moving the character around wherever. There is a second game following the story of Ellie and what happens at the end of the first game

  • Life is Strange
    • is a story telling game about how a girl manifests a power to rewind time a few seconds or going back in time through a polaroid photo. She goes on this journey to save her town and the people she love from a horrible typhoon she foresaw in her dreams. There are a few other games in the series, and all of them have a different story with different experiences and struggles with finding out about your powers and how to control them

  • Outlast
    • is a horror game based on a journalist going to a insane asylum to make a story, but gets trapped and realizes the hospital is a complete nightmare

  • Little Nightmares
    • is a game about a small boy who escapes huge monstrous and distorted humans and creatures. He meets another girl and they work together to escape what is coming after them

  • Resident Evil
    • is about a man named Leon Kennedy who is a police officer, and is currently on a mission to investigate the mysterious murders going on during a zombie outbreak. He has to survive against crazy strong creatures with supernatural powers. There are also multiple games to this series which all are very good

  • Until Dawn
    • is a horror storytelling game where a group of teenage friends go on vacation to a lodge in the mountains during the winter, where some strange phenomenoms start to happen and they suspect a killer is preying on them, but something far worse is upon them. The group has to survive until dawn when rescue is able to come

    And thats all! I hope you love these games as much as I do!
